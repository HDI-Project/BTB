Bases: object

add(X, y)[source]¶

Add data about known tunable hyperparameter configurations and scores. Refits model with all data. Args:

X: dict or list of dicts of hyperparameter combinations.
Keys may only be the name of a tunable, and the dictionary must contain values for all tunables.
y: float or list of floats of scores of the hyperparameter
combinations. Order of scores must match the order of the hyperparameter dictionaries that the scores corresponds
